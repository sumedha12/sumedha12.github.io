PROJECT DESCRIPTION:

I plan to make a job review site giving the feedback of all the IT companies. The feedback will be collected from employers working in the companies. It would help the new employers to come across and read the reviews. They can in turn also give there own feedback. The reviews will be on the basis of parameters like GROWTH,LEARNING, SALARY INCREMENTS,WORK ENVIRONMENT,HIGHER MANAGEMENT. I want to keep the focus of my site restricted to only IT companies
